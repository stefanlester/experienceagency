Healthcare cleaning jobs

Each and every healthcare facility requires cleaners. Experience Agenct Ltd are always looking for dedicated candidates to help stop the spread of infection, maintain sterile environments and ensure that everything's presentable for visitors and staff.

The importance of infection control

We understand the importance of effective cleaning and disinfection but also in finding the right person for the job. Our consultants allocate placements hospitals, clinics and surgeries throughout the country. Due to the environments that our candidates work in, Experience will always carry out an enhanced DBS check to ensure any applicants are suitable.
